The Mission

Mahi Productivity was built to solve a simple problem: The Internet is loud. This tool is designed for students and creators who need a "digital fortress" to get their best work done. It combines hardware-level logic with a soft, modern UI.

Installation & Security

As an independent developer, this app is not "digitally signed" by a major corporation. When you install:

  • Windows SmartScreen may appear. Click "More Info" and "Run Anyway".
  • The app requires Admin Privileges to function properly (this allows the App Blocker to close distracting programs).
  • Your data (Journal/Todo) is stored locally on your machine. We never see your data.
